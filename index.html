The actual story to rehearse

You enter a rib-cage opera house. The diaphragm frog drops the central dome-floor to start inspiration. Rib acrobats open the cage. On the left, neck and pec performers fix/lift the upper chest. On the right, serrated saw creatures adjust the posterior/lateral ribs. In the basement, abdominal warriors squeeze the air out; the lumbar lumberjack anchors rib 12; the lat swimmer finishes with a cough/pull-down.
