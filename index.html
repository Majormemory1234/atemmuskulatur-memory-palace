The story you rehearse

  1. The dome frog diaphragm drops the circus floor with a C3–5 collar.
  2. Intercostal rib acrobats spread the rib-ladder with Thor 1–11 plates.
  3. On the inhale side: the SCM sailor, scalene stair goblins, major/minor pec duo, and serratus saw creatures lift/fix the tent.
  4. On the exhale side: the rectus knight, transverse corset, oblique sash warriors, square lumbar lumberjack, and wide lat swimmer squeeze the air out.
