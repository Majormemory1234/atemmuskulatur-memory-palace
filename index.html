How to rehearse it

Start at the central diaphragm frog dropping the floor, then the rib acrobats expand the tent. Move to the left for inspiration helpers lifting/fixing the rib cage. Move to the right/bottom for forced expiration: abs, obliques, quadratus, and latissimus squeeze or pull the air out.
